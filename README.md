# Booker T. Washington WebTower · Alumni Business Cabinet

**School:** Booker T. Washington High School (Memphis, TN)  
**Motto:** _We Lead, Others Follow_  
**Grid:** Memphis DC · FlightLine Architecture  
**Pattern:** WebTower · Alumni Business UniVCity

This repo powers a **public, read-only WebTower** for BTW alumni businesses.

- The UI is a **trophy cabinet** of alumni founders, CEOs, and business owners.
- The left side is a **Map Window**, anchoring BTW to its real-world coordinates.
- The right side is the **Alumni Business Cabinet**, showing tiles for each alumni business.
- Everything is designed to sit inside the **Memphis DC / FlightLine** universe.
